Famous Religious Places

Kedarnath

Kedarnath

Famous for its ancient Shiva temple and Himalayan backdrop.

Best Time: May to October

Badrinath

Badrinath

One of the Char Dhams, dedicated to Lord Vishnu.

Best Time: May to October

Haridwar

Haridwar

A sacred city on the banks of the Ganga, known for Ganga Aarti.

Best Time: Throughout the year

Yamunotri

Yamunotri

The source of the Yamuna River, and a sacred temple of Goddess Yamuna.

Best Time: May to October

Gangotri

Gangotri

Origin of the holy Ganga river, a popular pilgrimage site.

Best Time: May to October

Rishikesh

Rishikesh

Known for its spiritual significance and the Ganga Aarti.

Best Time: September to March

Guptkashi

Guptkashi

Known for the Vishwanath Temple and its proximity to Kedarnath.

Best Time: May to October

Nainital

Nainital

Known for its Naina Devi Temple, perched by the beautiful Naini Lake.

Best Time: March to June

Panch Prayag

Panch Prayag

A sacred confluence of five rivers in Uttarakhand, considered highly holy.

Best Time: May to October

Meetha Reetha Sahib

Meetha Reetha Sahib

A revered Gurudwara located in the forests of Uttarakhand.

Best Time: Throughout the year

Kailash Mansarovar

Kailash Mansarovar

A revered sacred site for Hindus, Buddhists, Jains, and Tibetans.

Best Time: May to September

Jageshwar

Jageshwar

Known for its ancient temples, dedicated to Lord Shiva.

Best Time: September to April

Gwaldham

Gwaldham

A sacred pilgrimage site known for the temple of Goddess Galdham.

Best Time: May to October

Gopeshwar

Gopeshwar

Home to the Gopinath Temple dedicated to Lord Shiva.

Best Time: May to October
