Famous Wildlife Sanctuaries and Parks

Jim Corbett National Park

Jim Corbett National Park

India’s first national park, famous for its Bengal tigers.

Best Time: November to June

Rajaji National Park

Rajaji National Park

Known for its elephant population and rich biodiversity.

Best Time: November to June

Nanda Devi National Park

Nanda Devi National Park

A UNESCO World Heritage Site, home to rare wildlife species.

Best Time: May to October

Binsar Wildlife Sanctuary

Binsar Wildlife Sanctuary

Known for its dense forests, it’s a haven for wildlife lovers and birdwatchers.

Best Time: March to June

Kedarnath Wildlife Sanctuary

Kedarnath Wildlife Sanctuary

Located around the Kedarnath area, home to diverse flora and fauna.

Best Time: March to June

Sariska Tiger Reserve

Sariska Tiger Reserve

A protected area known for Bengal tigers and its diverse flora and fauna.

Best Time: October to March

Valley of Flowers National Park

Valley of Flowers National Park

A UNESCO World Heritage site, famous for its stunning array of wildflowers.

Best Time: July to September
